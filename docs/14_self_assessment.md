This project was an incredible deep dive into the real-world complexities of machine learning, moving far beyond simple notebook tutorials into a professional MLOps workflow. Aligning perfectly with my goal of becoming a Full-Stack Data Scientist, the task challenged me to manage a massive dataset of over 200,000 transactions and address a 28.09% churn rate through a containerized Docker environment. The difficulty level was significant, particularly when troubleshooting local kernel failures and transitioning to a robust docker-compose execution strategy, which served as a high-value lesson in environment isolation and reproducibility. By building a live FastAPI backend, I unlocked critical skills in API development and learned how to translate raw model metrics like ROC-AUC into meaningful business outcomes, such as protecting revenue through targeted retention. I used AI as a strategic collaborator throughout this process to brainstorm feature engineering ideas, debug complex pathing issues inside Docker, and refine my technical documentation, ensuring the final system was not just accurate, but production-ready.
