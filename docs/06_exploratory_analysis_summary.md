# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Summary

## Key Insights
1. **Peak Hours:** Most transactions occur between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM.
2. **Top Market:** The United Kingdom represents over 90% of total sales.
3. **Cancellations:** Approximately 10,206 transactions are cancellations (marked with 'C').
4. **Customer Concentration:** A small percentage of "Wholesale" style customers drive a large portion of the total quantity.
