# Business Impact Analysis

## Executive Summary
The implementation of the XGBoost Churn Prediction model provides a data-driven approach to customer retention. By shifting from reactive to proactive engagement, the business can significantly reduce the 28.09% churn rate identified in the initial analysis.

## Key Financial Metrics
- **Churn Rate:** 28.09%
- **Model Recall:** 71%
- **Targetable Revenue:** £1,404,500 at risk monthly.
- **Estimated Savings:** £179,560 per month (assuming a 20% success rate in retention campaigns).

## Strategic Recommendations
1. **Targeted Discounts:** Use the model's "High Risk" flags to send personalized 10% discount vouchers.
2. **Feedback Loops:** For customers flagged as "At Risk," trigger automated surveys to understand pain points before they leave.
3. **Resource Allocation:** Shift 60% of the marketing budget from broad acquisition to targeted retention of high-value (High RFM score) customers.
